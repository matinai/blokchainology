What is Web3 ?

Web 3 is the next generation of the Internet and a paradigm shift towards a democratic and decentralized Internet. Web 3 through new technologies such as "Blockchain", "Virtual Reality", "Augmented Reality", "Artificial Intelligence" and other technologies created in the field of cryptocurrencies such as "Metaverse" and "Non-Fungible Tokens" (NFT) are defined. By using these technologies, Web Three changes the users' view and value towards the Internet. It can be said that the topic of Web 3 is the construction of the people's Internet, which means that the people own the Internet and all its tools are designed to serve the people.
